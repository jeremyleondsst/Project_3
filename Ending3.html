Ending 3

I took the USB home, but it wouldn't work on my laptop. The next day at school, I borrowed one of their laptops to see what was in it. Pictures of me. So many pictures of me. I went ahead to report the fellow Edwin, although it was to no avail because it's been rumored that he moved back to Mexico. Bummer. After a few weeks, I totally forgot about the matter, but there was a knock on my door. I opened it, and someone who reminded of Edwin, but with more hair, stood there. He just stood there for half an hour. I asked how he knew where I lived, and he said he was my son, and before I could process any of that, he shot me cold blooded...
